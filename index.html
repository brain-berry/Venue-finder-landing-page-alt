❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VenueFinderNG

What is VenueFinderNG?

VenueFinderNG is a platform (mobile app and website) that connects venue owners with event planners and customers looking to book event spaces in Nigeria.

Who can use VenueFinderNG?

Anyone can use the platform whether you're a venue owner listing your property or an event planner searching for venues. All users must register and agree to our Terms & Conditions.

What am I allowed to do on the platform?

You can:

  • Create and manage listings
  • Browse available venues
  • Communicate with venue owners/planners
  • Make or accept bookings

You may not misuse the app, resell it, or tamper with the platform's design or code.

Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is optional for browsing, but required if you want to book or list venues.

What are the responsibilities of a venue owner?

Venue owners must:

  • Upload accurate and up-to-date listings
  • Respond to booking inquiries promptly
  • Ensure images, prices, and availability are correct

What behaviors are prohibited?

The following are not allowed:

  • Posting fake or misleading listings
  • Impersonating another user
  • Using the app for illegal activity
  • Uploading malware or attempting to hack the system
  • Promoting unrelated services

Violations may lead to suspension or account deletion without notice.

Does VenueFinderNG take a commission from bookings?

No. All transactions are between the event planner and the venue owner. VenueFinderNG does not collect commission unless stated otherwise in future updates.

What if there's a dispute between a planner and a venue owner?

VenueFinderNG is not liable for:

  • Booking failures
  • Disputes between users
  • Payments made outside the app
  • Event success or venue issues

We recommend resolving issues through direct communication.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. We only collect limited data (like your name, email, and phone number) to facilitate bookings. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Can my account be terminated?

Yes. Your account may be suspended or removed if:

  • You violate our terms
  • You upload false listings
  • We receive consistent user complaints
